\section{空間的な揺らぎ}

粒子集団のばらつき具合, 空間的な揺らぎを時系列で考える.

\begin{align}
  \sigma_{y} (t)
  &= \sqrt{\frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^{N} (y_i (t) - \bar{y_i}(t) )^2} \\
  &= \sqrt{\frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^{N} (y_i (t) - Y_g (t) )^2} \\
  &= \sqrt{\frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^{N} {{y_i} (t)}^2 - {{Y_g} (t)}^2}
\end{align}
